Murder on The Orient Express

A lavish trip through Europe quickly unfolds into a race against time to solve a murder aboard a train. When an avalanche stops the Orient Express dead in its tracks, the world's greatest detective arrives to interrogate all passengers and search for clues before the killer can strike again.
